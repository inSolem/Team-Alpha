Level up your farm's productivity with our IOT solutions

The Alpha System is an IoT-based Smart Agriculture System which aims to enhance the productivity of the aquaculture sector.

It was developed to solve the problems arising from manual monitoring process, which can be laborious, time-consuming and inefficient.

Combining the state-of-the-art sensors and cloud technology, the Alpha System provides 24/7 real-time updates and notifications on the oxygen level, pH level and temperature of the water.

This enables timely intervention and rectification for the farmers to provide the most optimal environment to grow their best quality crops ever.

State-of-the-Art Sensors

The sensors are able to provide accurate measurements of the oxygen, temperature and pH level of the water

Data at a Glance

Data are beautifully visualised on a dashboard for easy data comparison and abnormalities detection

Real-Time Monitoring and Updates

Users will receive real-time notifications via Telegram when a certain threshold is hit

Our solutions


Dissolved Oxygen Sensor

Measures O2 Level of the water.

pH sensor

Measures pH level of the water. Different marine life thrive under different pH environment. With accurate measurements, farmers can ensure the most optimal environment for the marine life to thrive.

Temperature sensor

Measures the temperature of the water. Marine life are sensitive to the water temperature as a less than ideal temperature may cause stress on them.

Turbidity sensor

Marine do not survive well in dirty water. Our Turbidity solution meassures the ability of light to penetrate into the water. Low penetration rate indicate poor water quality.

Real-Time Data Dashboard

The dashboard provides real-time updates of the measurements from the sensors.

Real-Time Notifications

When the sensors detect abnormalities or reach a certain threshold set by the user, the user will receive an instant notification on their mobile phone. This allows instant rectification of the situation to prevent a potential crisis from happening.
